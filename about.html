ABOUT UNITY COLLECTIVE

Unity Collective is a platform that showcases past Emerge projects created by Ara students. Our goal is to highlight creative, innovative, and well-executed design projects that support students.

OUR MISSION

To provide a creative outlet hub for students to showcase and represent their work to the world.

OUR VISION

To build a strong community where students collaborate, share knowledge, and elevate their talents.

WHAT WE DO

We collect, organize, and present student projects from various disciplines so they can reach, and get inspired.
